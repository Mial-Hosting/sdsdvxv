Our Goal

Our goal is to implement a sustainable energy solution for our school by installing solar panels on the roof. By harnessing solar power, we aim to reduce our carbon footprint and promote environmental responsibility within our community. This initiative not only benefits the school by providing renewable energy for our computers and lighting but also serves as an educational opportunity to inspire future generations about the importance of renewable energy sources. Together, we're working towards a greener and more sustainable future for our school and beyond.

Cost structure and benefits

Cost:

Solarpanels: 30pc at 700 per solar panel -> 21.000 EURO
Montage: 3 days of work for 2 workers -> 3.000 EURO


Benefits:

Cost Savings: By using solar energy, we reduce our reliance on other electricity sources, leading to long-term cost savings on our school energy bills.

Environmental Impact: Using solar power reduces our carbon footprint, helping to combat climate change.

Educational Value: The installation of solar panels provides an educational opportunity for students to learn about renewable energy in class.

Reputation: Our commitment to sustainable energy practices enhances our school's reputation as an environmentally school.

Flexibility: Solar power increases our energy flexibility by diversifying our energy sources and reducing our dependence on external energy providers.

Long-Term Investment: While there is an one time investment in installing solar panels, they offer long-term benefits, including reduced energy costs and potential revenue generation through selling the energy to companys like "Salzburg AG".
